ÜBER MICH

I am Weijie Ring (nee Zhao), an Associate Research Fellow, German literature scholar, and translator. My work is driven by a fascination with the intersection of literature and dance, modern German literature, and the intricate nuances of poetic rhythm.

To date, I have contributed articles to international journals such as Euphorion, Athenäum, and the Hoffmann-Jahrbuch, and I am the author of the monograph Dance in Literature (De Gruyter, 2022). Beyond the world of research, I collaborate as a translator with the Frankfurt Book Fair. My background in vocal music, dance, and elocution informs my work, allowing me to bring a dynamic, sensory perspective to both my literary interpretation and my teaching.
